Steady Rest

Arma Automotive utilizes a CNC Tube Notcher to manufacture tube designs. The existing design for the tube holding device did not allow free rotation of the held tube and provided poor accuracy of cuts due to the mathematical inaccuracy of the centerline at various diameters of tubes. My redesign solved these problems, and implemented a wider diameter range, with easier tube loading.

My redesigned version provided all of the following features:

  • Smooth tube rotation from implementing roller bearings.
  • Mathematically accurate constant centers across diameters of tube from 1 to 2.5 inches.
  • Improved material loading features such as a multi-stepped latch.
  • Smoother operation of the slider mechanisms.

As an added advantage, the design is fully manufacturable with sheet metal parts and off the shelf components. This allowed for the cost effective production of the product as a whole.
