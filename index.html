Experience

GiBLI Tech

During my time at GiBLI Tech, I was involved in the design of an aerodynamic sensing system for cyclists. In addition to the design of the windspeed probe, I participated in all aspects of the mechanical design of the consumer product itself.

ARMA Automotive Steady Rest

Arma Automotive utilizes a CNC Tube Notcher to manufacture tube designs. The existing design for the tube holding device did not allow free rotation of the held tube and provided poor accuracy of cuts due to the mathematical inaccuracy of the centerline at various diameters of tubes. My redesign solved these problems, and implemented a wider diameter range, with easier tube loading.

ARMA Automotive Windshield Wipers

The design of the windshield wiper system for the Arma GT required conforming to regulations, which allow for the registration of the completed vehicle, while also designing within the constraints of the finalized car. This posed extreme space constraints, and limited mounting position availability. Despite the challenging constraints, a design was created allowing for both wipers to be driven from a singular motor.
